This data was collected from a project titled “Belly Button Biodiversity” which was started in 2011 by Dan Fergus and Sarah Council. It started as a small project but quickly gained popularity with many across North America. So much so that they had to stop accepting samples in 2012!

This study, while unnerving to some, displays the amazing biodiversity of our own bodies. Click the link below to learn more about this study and explore the raw data that populates this dashboard.
